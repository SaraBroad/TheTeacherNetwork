About Us

The Teacher Network was founded in 2017 as a way to forge a connection between the classroom and the community. As Philadelphia community members with extensive experience in education, both in the classroom and in non-profit organizations, we saw the need for a forum where businesses, non-profits, and other entities in Philadelphia can interact to build strong relationships to increase opportunities for Philadelphia school children.
